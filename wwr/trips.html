Follow these tips

  • Drink plenty of water
  • Apply sunscreen often
  • Wear hat and sunglasses (if possible)
  • No Alcohol or Illicit Drugs
  • Listen to your tour guide
  • Always Wear Safety Equipment
  • Bring a camera
  • Bring a waterproof bag
  • Always wear shoes
  • Never stand up in the river
  • Never go off by yourself
  • HAVE FUN!!!!!

Additional Information

  • Food and water provided for all trips
  • Be aware of what you do with human waste
  • Group discounts
  • Discounts for "Dry Oar" members
